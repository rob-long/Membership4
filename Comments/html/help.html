Comments

The Comments provides system-wide access to comments that have been posted in various places. This is useful for various purposes, such as:

  • Summarizing recent comments received on a site, regardless of whether they are posted in forums, blogs, or elsewhere.
  • Moderating new comments in a single place, rather than having to go to different plugins depending on where they were posted.

Moderation

When you launch the Comments plug-in, it displays all new comments awaiting moderation. The buttons to moderate are at the bottom. (Click "Goto bottom" to jump there):

  • Approve & publish - the selected comments are suitable for publishing on your site
  • Reject - the selected comments should not be published. Rejected comments are not deleted immediately, so by choosing this option, you can in principle change your mind later, or simply keep the rejected comment on file in case you want to archive abusive posts.
  • Delete - these are not good comments, and can be discarded immediately.

Check off all comments that should have the same moderation, and then click the appropriate moderation button. Use check all and uncheck all to quickly select or unselect all comments.
